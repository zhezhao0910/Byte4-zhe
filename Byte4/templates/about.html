Accountability: About This Data Set

This is the location data of a CMU student, from 10/4/2018 to 10/16/2018.

Where did this data come from?

This is the mobile data collected from CMU student, Zhe Zhao's iphone6s. In this study, the app 'AWARE' is used for data collection.

Who has access to it?

The data is owned by Zhe Zhao (both the researcher and the data provider) and she allows the public to access the data.
