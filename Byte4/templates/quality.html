The data is correct within 500 meters measure range, but incorrect out of 500 meters.The data shows inaccurate locations. This should be the sleep time, and she couldn't appear in street B.

This is the measure bias was brought by the inaccuracy of GPS data collection. The source of inaccuracy may somewhat be brought by the reseacher because the "location measure accuracy" is manually set as 150 metres by the researcher, but the actual inaccuracy is more than 400 meters.
